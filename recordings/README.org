Regarding the copyrights of the licks in this directory:

- "Wade in the water", according to Wikipedia, is an "African American
  jubilee song", whose lyrics were first published in 1901

- "The Saint Louis Blues" (or "St. Louis Blues"), according to
  Wikipedia, is a popular American song composed by W. C. Handy and
  published in September 1914.

The actual recordings in this directory are only one short lick from
each song and were done by me (Marc Ihm) on an A-Harmonica.
